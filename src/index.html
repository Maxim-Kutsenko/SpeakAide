What is a teleprompter app, and how does it work?

Teleprompter app - SpeakAide- works by displaying the text that the speaker or presenter wants to read on the screen in front of them. The text scrolls up at a pace that can be adjusted by the user. The speaker or presenter can read the text while looking directly into the camera or at a nearby screen, giving the impression of natural eye contact with the audience.

How does the teleprompter application help users improve their public speaking skills?

Using a teleprompter can help reduce anxiety and nervousness during public speaking because the speaker does not have to worry about memorizing a script or forgetting their lines. The speaker can maintain a consistent pace and avoid stumbling over their words. This can help improve the overall delivery and flow of their speech or presentation. SpeakAide can help speakers maintain eye contact by allowing them to read their notes while still looking directly at the audience.
